Pre-Algebra Answer Key - Set 3

Chapter 11: Addition & Subtraction of Fractions

EASY
Calculate: 15 + 35
Answer: 45
Explanation: Numerators are added directly as denominators are the same.
MEDIUM
Calculate: 34 - 16
Answer: 712
Explanation: LCM of 4 and 6 is 12. Convert fractions: 912 - 212 = 712.
HARD
Calculate: 2 13 + 1 12 - 56
Answer: 3
Explanation: Convert to improper fractions: 73 + 32 - 56. Common denominator is 6: 146 + 96 - 56 = 186 = 3.

Chapter 12: Word Problems with Fractions

EASY
Maria ate 14 of a pizza and John ate 14 of the same pizza. What fraction of the pizza did they eat in total?
Answer: 12
Explanation: 14 + 14 = 24. Simplify 24 to 12.
MEDIUM
A recipe needs 23 cup of flour. If you only have 14 cup, how much more flour do you need?
Answer: 512 cup
Explanation: Subtract 14 from 23. LCM of 3 and 4 is 12. 812 - 312 = 512 cup.
HARD
A baker uses 1 34 kg of sugar for a cake and 2 12 kg for cookies. If he started with 5 kg of sugar, how much sugar is left?
Answer: 34 kg
Explanation: Total sugar used: 1 34 + 2 12 = 74 + 52 = 74 + 104 = 174 kg. Sugar left: 5 - 174 = 204 - 174 = 34 kg.

Chapter 13: Multiplication of Fractions

EASY
Calculate: 12 × 35
Answer: 310
Explanation: Multiply numerators (1×3=3) and denominators (2×5=10).
MEDIUM
Calculate: 4 × 23
Answer: 83 or 2 23
Explanation: Treat 4 as 41. (41) × (23) = 83.
HARD
Calculate: 1 12 × 2 13
Answer: 72 or 3 12
Explanation: Convert to improper fractions: (32) × (73) = 216. Simplify by dividing by 3: 72.

Chapter 14: More Word Problems with Fractions

EASY
What is 13 of 12 apples?
Answer: 4 apples
Explanation: (13) × 12 = 123 = 4 apples.
MEDIUM
A garden is 34 meters long and 25 meters wide. What is the area of the garden in square meters?
Answer: 310 square meters
Explanation: Area = length × width = (34) × (25) = 620. Simplify to 310 square meters.
HARD
Tom had a chocolate bar. He ate 13 of it. He then gave 12 of the REMAINING part to his sister. What fraction of the original chocolate bar did his sister get?
Answer: 13 of the original bar
Explanation: Remaining after Tom ate: 1 - 13 = 23. Sister got: (12) × (23) = 26 = 13 of the original bar.

Chapter 15: Further Multiplication & Division of Fractions

EASY
Calculate: 3 ÷ 14
Answer: 12
Explanation: To divide by a fraction, multiply by its reciprocal: 3 × 41 = 12.
MEDIUM
Calculate: 23 ÷ 34
Answer: 89
Explanation: (23) × (43) = 89.
HARD
A piece of ribbon is 2 12 meters long. How many 14 meter pieces can be cut from it?
Answer: 10 pieces
Explanation: Convert 2 12 to 52. Then, (52) ÷ (14) = (52) × (41) = 202 = 10 pieces.
