Module 3: Greatest Common Factor

EASY
Find GCF of 12 and 18
Answer: 6
Common factors: 1, 2, 3, 6. Greatest is 6
MEDIUM
Find GCF of 24, 36, and 48
Answer: 12
Prime factors: 24=2³×3, 36=2²×3², 48=2⁴×3. GCF=2²×3=12
HARD
Find GCF of 84, 126, and 168
Answer: 42
Prime factors method: GCF = 2×3×7 = 42

Module 5: Least Common Multiple

EASY
Find LCM of 4 and 6
Answer: 12
Multiples of 4: 4,8,12... Multiples of 6: 6,12... First common is 12
MEDIUM
Find LCM of 8, 12, and 16
Answer: 48
Prime factors: 8=2³, 12=2²×3, 16=2⁴. LCM=2⁴×3=48
HARD
Find LCM of 15, 20, and 25
Answer: 300
Prime factors: 15=3×5, 20=2²×5, 25=5². LCM=2²×3×5²=300
