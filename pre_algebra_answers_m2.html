Module 7: Addition of Fractions

EASY
Add: 15 + 35
Answer: 45
Add numerators directly since denominators are the same.
MEDIUM
Add: 14 + 23
Answer: 1112

HARD
Add: 112 + 234
Answer: 414
Convert to improper fractions: 3/2 + 11/4 = 6/4 + 11/4 = 17/4 = 4 1/4.

Module 8: Subtraction of Fractions

EASY
Subtract: 78 - 38
Answer: 12
Subtract numerators directly: 4/8, then simplify to 1/2.
MEDIUM
Subtract: 56 - 13
Answer: 12
Common denominator is 6: 5/6 - 2/6 = 3/6 = 1/2.
HARD
Subtract: 314 - 112
Answer: 134
Convert to improper fractions: 13/4 - 3/2 = 13/4 - 6/4 = 7/4 = 1 3/4.

Module 9: Addition & Subtraction of Fractions

EASY
Solve: 27 + 47 - 17
Answer: 57
Perform operations in order: 6/7 - 1/7 = 5/7.
MEDIUM
Solve: 13 + 12 - 16
Answer: 23
Common denominator is 6: 2/6 + 3/6 - 1/6 = 5/6 - 1/6 = 4/6 = 2/3.
HARD
A recipe calls for 34 cup of flour and 12 cup of sugar. If you use 18 cup of butter, how much more flour and sugar combined is needed than butter?
Answer: 118 cup
Flour + sugar = 3/4 + 1/2 = 3/4 + 2/4 = 5/4. Difference = 5/4 - 1/8 = 10/8 - 1/8 = 9/8 = 1 1/8.

Module 10: Word Problems with Fractions

EASY
John ate 14 of a pizza. Sarah ate 24 of the same pizza. What fraction of the pizza did they eat in total?
Answer: 34
Add the fractions: 1/4 + 2/4 = 3/4.
MEDIUM
A farmer planted corn on 13 of his field and soybeans on 12 of his field. What fraction of his field is still unplanted?
Answer: 16
Total planted: 1/3 + 1/2 = 2/6 + 3/6 = 5/6. Unplanted: 1 - 5/6 = 1/6.
HARD
Emily spent 13 of her money on a book and 14 of her money on a pen. If she had $10 left, how much money did she start with?
